World Happiness Report

Headlines for 2015

The World Happiness Report is an annual publication of the United Nations Sustainable Development Solutions Network. It contains articles, and rankings of national happiness based on respondent ratings of their own lives, which the report also correlates with various life factors. As of March 2015, Switzerland was ranked the happiest country in the world.

Switzerland tops the happiness rankings for the third year in a row Denmark, Finland, Iceland & Norway complete the top 5 Cities are ranked for the first time; the happiest city in the world is Helsinki
